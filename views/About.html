Herrods Munchies

Welcome to Herrods Munchies! The application gives the user suggestions of possible dishes to cook according to the ingredients the user has and the means of the kitchen at home. When logging in, the user will have the option to edit the ingredients in his hands, and at the click of a button he will receive suggestions for possible dishes to prepare for him. When choosing a dish, the application will automatically update the inventory of the ingredients in the hands of the user. .
